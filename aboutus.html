About us -Sascha and Lars

Our Story

Why do two Germans move to Costa Rica? Already during our first holiday together in Denmark, we realized that a house on the beach is the goal of our lives. Different, as we are, both of us - Lars was a software developer in Germany and Sascha was a teacher - had this goal that forged us together.

But where should the journey go? Mallorca, Southern Spain, Greece? All fascinating destinations!
But already in the first holiday in Costa Rica we were sure: here we want to live - self-determined. During our travels across the country, we found our beautiful house in Playa Coyote and felt right at home. The air, the ocean, the people and the attitude to life immediately captured us here.

Soon the desire began to mature in us to become good hosts. On the one hand, of course, to make money here, but above all to meet people from all over the world and to give you a nice time with us. In 2014 we started renting our cottage to holidaymakers. Our guests were, at least they told us and wrote it in reviews, immediately loved our house and felt comfortable here.


We really treat every guest as a guest. This is what distinguishes us. Additionally, we always try something new. Be it our homemade German bread, that we also like to share with our guests, or our own honey, wine or small presents. We want our guests to feel the way they are for us. Something special.
