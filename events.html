Events at Playa Coyote

Playa Coyote hosts a number of unique events. Take part in the 4x4 Rally in October, the Mountain bike and Enduro race in January or visit one of the regular offers in the area.

We are happy to inform you about the events and how you can participate. Tours can be booked through us. Use the contact form at the bottom of this page. Enjoy the wild life.
